ABOUT ME

I believe that technology can improve lives and help others, as it can open up opportunities to make everyday tasks easier and more efficient.

Back in April 2017 I decided to follow my passion in technology, where I have recently completed the full time General Assembly course in web development. My passion is now stronger than it has ever been and I am eager to upskill myself further and work alongside talented developers.

I have received training in:

Javascript | React | CSS | HTML | jQuey | Rails | Sass | Bootstrap | API's | Ajax | Git | Github | Command Line | Pair Programming | Test Driven Development | Agile Processesses

My past experience in customer service management taught many great skills, including communication, working under pressure and being adaptable to various situations. I have a loyal work history and my personal goal for any job is to continuously learn and share knowledge.

In my spare time, aside from learning I am an avid reader. Once I pick up a book I cannot put it down and am known to finish books within a few days. Reading has led to a passion for writing fiction, although I haven't completed anything yet, a bucket list goal is to have a book of mine published one day.
